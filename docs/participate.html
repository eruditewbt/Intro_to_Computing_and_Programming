Join and act

Learning gets real when you participate in a working community.

We now have active repos and project lanes beyond the foundations repo. Your job is not only to read. It is to join the community, pick the right repo, ask for access where needed, and start contributing to something real.

Hands-on

Activity: join the community properly

1 Join Discord and subscribe on YouTube.
2 Open the repo that matches your current path.
3 Post your intent: name, field, what you want to build, and what help you need.
4 Give your email so you can be granted access to collaboration repos when needed.
5 Start with one small contribution, not a huge promise.
